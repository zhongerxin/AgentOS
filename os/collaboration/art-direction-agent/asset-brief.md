# Asset Brief: Texas Hold'em Table

## Tone Direction

Default direction: polished but friendly. Avoid a dark casino-heavy look; the app should feel like a playable social game inside ChatGPT.

## Needed Assets

| Asset | Priority | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Table felt texture | High | Should work behind text and cards. |
| Card faces | High | Must be legible at small iframe sizes. |
| Card backs | High | One distinctive design. |
| Chip stacks | Medium | Three denominations are enough for MVP. |
| Avatars | Medium | Three bot opponents with recognizable silhouettes. |
| Dealer and blind markers | Medium | Small but readable. |
| Win-state banner | Low | Can be simple for prototype. |

## Constraints

- UI will run inside a ChatGPT iframe.
- Assets should be optimized for quick load.
- Avoid copyrighted casino or playing-card brand references.
