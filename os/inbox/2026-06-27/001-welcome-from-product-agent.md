Hi,

For the first playable version, I recommend optimizing for a fast hand loop rather than a full poker room simulation.

Suggested MVP:

1. Player joins a single table.
2. Two bot opponents are seated automatically.
3. The app deals hole cards, runs preflop/flop/turn/river betting, and shows a showdown.
4. ChatGPT can explain why an action is legal or illegal.
5. The UI keeps the game state visible while the model narrates only the important moments.

Open question: should bots be purely deterministic at first, or should they have lightweight personalities?
